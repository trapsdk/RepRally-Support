Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I restore purchases?
    If you're using a new device, open the app, go to the Subscription section, and tap "Restore Purchases".
  • I’m having trouble logging in.
    Make sure you're using the correct email address. If you're still stuck, email us with your account details.
  • How do I cancel my subscription?
    You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple ID settings under Subscriptions.
